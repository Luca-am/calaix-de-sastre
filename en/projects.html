Antropoblue

Specialised social analysis tool for the BlueSky ecosystem that combines quantitative data and qualitative interpretation. It automatically gathers relevant posts, offers thematic dashboards, filters by author, language, dates and interactions, and helps detect emerging trends. It includes ethnographic note-taking features that enrich the analysis with contextual observations. Designed for researchers, journalists, NGOs and companies that need to understand social conversations with methodological rigour and private datasets.

Etnoritme

Etnoritme is a web/blog that investigates social issues through songs and the surrounding social, cultural and political context. Using albums, music videos and articles related to artists and their music as empirical material, it analyses narratives and ideas that emerge to show how they interact with and shape social debates and practices, revealing the dynamics and processes that form contemporary culture.

TFG

Hybrid ethnography — virtual and in-person — of Operación Triunfo. It analyses interactions on social media (#OT2025) and weekly fan gatherings to watch the galas, in order to understand how parasocial relationships and the programme structure articulate neoliberal narratives based on competitiveness, self-improvement and personal branding, shared and reinterpreted by fans across physical and digital spaces.
